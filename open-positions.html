Summer Internship

  • Contact Person: Prof. Gerry Lacey
  • Email: geryy.lacey@mu.ie

Are you passionate about robotics, agriculture, or cutting-edge technology? Join our AURA team for an exciting Summer Internship opportunity in the field of Agricultural Robotics.

Description:

We are seeking motivated and enthusiastic individuals to work on one or more of the following domains i.e. Autonomous Navigation, Computer Vision, Selective Harvesting. This internship will provide hands-on experience, exposure to advanced technologies, and the chance to collaborate with a dynamic team of researchers.

Eligibility:
  • Currently pursuing a degree in Electronics, Robotics, Computer Science, Mechanical Engineering, or a related field.
  • Strong programming skills (e.g., Python, ROS, or C++).
  • Basic understanding of sensors, actuators, and hardware integration.

    • Salary:
  • Senior Research Fellow (2023): €73,589 p.a. (1 point - with increment).
  • Research Fellow (2023): €61,318 p.a. – €66,815 p.a. (4 points).

    • How to Apply: Please send your:
  • Resume/CV
  • A brief statement of interest (max 300 words)
  • Optional: Links to GitHub/Portfolio/Relevant Projects
    • to Prof. Gerry Lacey (Email: gerry.lacey@mu.ie) with the subject line: Summer Internship Application – AURA Project.
